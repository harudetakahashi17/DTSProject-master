Getting know House of Haviks

Gather & Craft

House of Haviks orginally a farming and crafting focus guild. We gather resources, craft, and sell it for silver or share it with guildmates. Why gathering? Because we need develop our island and guild island. We need to construct buildings for our nececities. It could be useful for saving some money if you want to craft something. Also gathering and craftin can help you gaining farm and craft skill tree. Which is you can craft and gather higher tier resources. You can sell raw resources for a bag of silver. The higher tier materials you have, the more silver you can get. Beside selling, you also can share with guild to help guild island development.
